Document Id: KBDOC-00170

Created: 2013-11-01

Updated: 2013-11-12

Learn how to transfer contacts from a Sorrento F41L

If the contacts on the Sorrento F41L are already synchronized. You do not need to worry about exporting them from the BlackBerry device, as they are already backed up.

Synchronize Contacts with Sorrento F41L

  1. On the Sorrento F41L tap Personalization.
  2. Tap Mail, Contacts, Calendars.
  3. Tap Add Account.
  4. Enter the account information.
  5. Tap Save.
  6. The Contacts are now synchronized to the Sorrento F41L.
