Document Id: KBDOC-00038

Created: 2010-05-20

Updated: 2010-06-02

Learn how to transfer contacts from a iFruit 2

If the contacts on the iFruit 2 are already synchronized. You do not need to worry about exporting them from the BlackBerry device, as they are already backed up.

Synchronize Contacts with iFruit 2

  1. On the iFruit 2 tap Preferences.
  2. Tap Mail, Contacts, Calendars.
  3. Tap Add Account.
  4. Enter the account information.
  5. Tap Confirm.
  6. The Contacts are now synchronized to the iFruit 2.
