Learn how to back up MeeToo 3.0

Things are backed up to The Cloud! You'll never lose anything!

Some things that are backed up MeeToo 3.0

  • Your call history.
  • Your theme color.
  • The accounts you've set up on your phone.
  • Your places.

To back up MeeToo 3.0

  1. On the MeeToo 3.0 tap Settings.
  2. Tap Backup.
