Updated: 2013-02-21

Learn how to transfer contacts from a iFruit 4A

If the contacts on the iFruit 4A are already synchronized. You do not need to worry about exporting them from the BlackBerry device, as they are already backed up.

Synchronize Contacts with iFruit 4A

  1. On the iFruit 4A tap Personalization.
  2. Tap Mail, Contacts, Calendars.
  3. Tap Add Account.
  4. Enter the account information.
  5. Tap Add.
  6. The Contacts are now synchronized to the iFruit 4A.
