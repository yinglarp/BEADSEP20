Sorrento F32L Battery Life

Just like any other electric device, the Sorrento F32L must be charged. If it seems to be discharging quickly, you are using the phone too much.

Charging the phone

  • Rub a baloon on your head and discharge the charge on the phone.
  • Find an electric eel.
  • Use the Raiden Lightning move to charge the phone instantaneously.
