Learn how to transfer contacts from a Ronin S2

If the contacts on the Ronin S2 are already synchronized. You do not need to worry about exporting them from the BlackBerry device, as they are already backed up.

Synchronize Contacts with Ronin S2

  1. On the Ronin S2 tap Preferences.
  2. Tap Mail, Contacts, Calendars.
  3. Tap Add Account.
  4. Enter the account information.
  5. Tap Confirm.
  6. The Contacts are now synchronized to the Ronin S2.
