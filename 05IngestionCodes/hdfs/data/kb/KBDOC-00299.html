iFruit 5A Battery Life

Just like any other electric device, the iFruit 5A must be charged. If it seems to be discharging quickly, you are using the phone too much.

Charging the phone

  • Rub a baloon on your head and discharge the charge on the phone.
  • Tie to kite during a thunderstorm.
  • Use the Raiden Lightning move to charge the phone instantaneously.
