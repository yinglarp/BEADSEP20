Titanic 2300 - Change the phone ringtone and notification sound

Document Id: KBDOC-00121

Created: 2011-02-18

Updated: 2011-02-19

Steps to change the phone ringtone for calls on your Titanic 2300

  1. From the Preferences screen, tap Preferences.
  2. Tap Sounds.
  3. Tap Ringtone.
  4. Tap your preferred ringtone.
  5. You've now changed the ringtone.

Steps to change the notification ringtone for texts on Titanic 2300

  1. From the Preferences screen, tap Preferences.
  2. Tap Sounds.
  3. Tap Text Tone.
  4. Tap your preferred ringtone.
  5. You've now changed the ringtone.

Steps to change the notification ringtone for emails on Titanic 2300

  1. From the Preferences screen, tap Preferences.
  2. Tap Sounds.
  3. Scroll to and tap New Mail.
  4. Tap your preferred ringtone.
  5. You've now changed the ringtone.
