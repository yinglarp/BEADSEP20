Learn how to transfer contacts from a Titanic 2500

If the contacts on the Titanic 2500 are already synchronized. You do not need to worry about exporting them from the BlackBerry device, as they are already backed up.

Synchronize Contacts with Titanic 2500

  1. On the Titanic 2500 tap Settings.
  2. Tap Mail, Contacts, Calendars.
  3. Tap Add Account.
  4. Enter the account information.
  5. Tap Save.
  6. The Contacts are now synchronized to the Titanic 2500.
