Learn how to transfer contacts from a Titanic 4000

If the contacts on the Titanic 4000 are already synchronized. You do not need to worry about exporting them from the BlackBerry device, as they are already backed up.

Synchronize Contacts with Titanic 4000

  1. On the Titanic 4000 tap Preferences.
  2. Tap Mail, Contacts, Calendars.
  3. Tap Add Account.
  4. Enter the account information.
  5. Tap Done.
  6. The Contacts are now synchronized to the Titanic 4000.
