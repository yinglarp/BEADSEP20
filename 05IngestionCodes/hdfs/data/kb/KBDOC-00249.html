Learn how to back up Sorrento F40L

Things are backed up to The Cloud! You'll never lose anything!

Some things that are backed up Sorrento F40L

  • Your call history.
  • Your theme color.
  • The accounts you've set up on your phone.
  • Your bookmarks.

To back up Sorrento F40L

  1. On the Sorrento F40L tap Preferences.
  2. Tap Backup.
