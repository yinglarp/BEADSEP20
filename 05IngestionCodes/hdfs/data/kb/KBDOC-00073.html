Sorrento F23L - Change the phone ringtone and notification sound

Document Id: KBDOC-00073

Created: 2011-11-21

Updated: 2011-12-02

Steps to change the phone ringtone for calls on your Sorrento F23L

  1. From the Preferences screen, tap Settings.
  2. Tap Sounds.
  3. Tap Ringtone.
  4. Tap your preferred ringtone.
  5. You've now changed the ringtone.

Steps to change the notification ringtone for texts on Sorrento F23L

  1. From the Preferences screen, tap Settings.
  2. Tap Sounds.
  3. Tap Text Tone.
  4. Tap your preferred ringtone.
  5. You've now changed the ringtone.

Steps to change the notification ringtone for emails on Sorrento F23L

  1. From the Preferences screen, tap Settings.
  2. Tap Sounds.
  3. Scroll to and tap New Mail.
  4. Tap your preferred ringtone.
  5. You've now changed the ringtone.
