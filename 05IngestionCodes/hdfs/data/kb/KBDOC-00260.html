Learn how to transfer contacts from a Sorrento F11L

If the contacts on the Sorrento F11L are already synchronized. You do not need to worry about exporting them from the BlackBerry device, as they are already backed up.

Synchronize Contacts with Sorrento F11L

  1. On the Sorrento F11L tap Settings.
  2. Tap Mail, Contacts, Calendars.
  3. Tap Add Account.
  4. Enter the account information.
  5. Tap Confirm.
  6. The Contacts are now synchronized to the Sorrento F11L.
