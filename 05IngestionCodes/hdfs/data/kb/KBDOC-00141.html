Learn how to back up Sorrento F32L

Things are backed up to The Cloud! You'll never lose anything!

Some things that are backed up Sorrento F32L

  • Your call history.
  • Your theme color.
  • The accounts you've set up on your phone.
  • Your favorites.

To back up Sorrento F32L

  1. On the Sorrento F32L tap Preferences.
  2. Tap Backup.
