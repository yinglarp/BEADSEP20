Updated: 2012-08-13

Learn how to transfer contacts from a Titanic 3000

If the contacts on the Titanic 3000 are already synchronized. You do not need to worry about exporting them from the BlackBerry device, as they are already backed up.

Synchronize Contacts with Titanic 3000

  1. On the Titanic 3000 tap Settings.
  2. Tap Mail, Contacts, Calendars.
  3. Tap Add Account.
  4. Enter the account information.
  5. Tap Add.
  6. The Contacts are now synchronized to the Titanic 3000.
