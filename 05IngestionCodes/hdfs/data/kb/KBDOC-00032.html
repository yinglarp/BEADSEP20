iFruit 3 - Transfer Contacts

Document Id: KBDOC-00032

Created: 2011-11-02

Updated: 2011-11-09

Learn how to transfer contacts from a iFruit 3

If the contacts on the iFruit 3 are already synchronized. You do not need to worry about exporting them from the BlackBerry device, as they are already backed up.

Synchronize Contacts with iFruit 3

  1. On the iFruit 3 tap Personalization.
  2. Tap Mail, Contacts, Calendars.
  3. Tap Add Account.
  4. Enter the account information.
  5. Tap Done.
  6. The Contacts are now synchronized to the iFruit 3.
