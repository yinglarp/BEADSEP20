Sorrento F32L - Change the phone ringtone and notification sound

Document Id: KBDOC-00139

Created: 2012-10-21

Updated: 2012-10-29

Steps to change the phone ringtone for calls on your Sorrento F32L

  1. From the Preferences screen, tap Preferences.
  2. Tap Sounds.
  3. Tap Ringtone.
  4. Tap your preferred ringtone.
  5. You've now changed the ringtone.

Steps to change the notification ringtone for texts on Sorrento F32L

  1. From the Preferences screen, tap Preferences.
  2. Tap Sounds.
  3. Tap Text Tone.
  4. Tap your preferred ringtone.
  5. You've now changed the ringtone.

Steps to change the notification ringtone for emails on Sorrento F32L

  1. From the Preferences screen, tap Preferences.
  2. Tap Sounds.
  3. Scroll to and tap New Mail.
  4. Tap your preferred ringtone.
  5. You've now changed the ringtone.
