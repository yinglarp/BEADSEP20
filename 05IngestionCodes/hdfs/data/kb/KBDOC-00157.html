Sorrento F10L - Change the phone ringtone and notification sound

Document Id: KBDOC-00157

Created: 2009-10-25

Updated: 2009-11-07

Steps to change the phone ringtone for calls on your Sorrento F10L

  1. From the Settings screen, tap Preferences.
  2. Tap Sounds.
  3. Tap Ringtone.
  4. Tap your preferred ringtone.
  5. You've now changed the ringtone.

Steps to change the notification ringtone for texts on Sorrento F10L

  1. From the Settings screen, tap Preferences.
  2. Tap Sounds.
  3. Tap Text Tone.
  4. Tap your preferred ringtone.
  5. You've now changed the ringtone.

Steps to change the notification ringtone for emails on Sorrento F10L

  1. From the Settings screen, tap Preferences.
  2. Tap Sounds.
  3. Scroll to and tap New Mail.
  4. Tap your preferred ringtone.
  5. You've now changed the ringtone.
