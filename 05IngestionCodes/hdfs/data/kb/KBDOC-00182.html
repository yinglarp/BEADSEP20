MeeToo 4.1 - Transfer Contacts

Document Id: KBDOC-00182

Created: 2012-08-04

Updated: 2012-08-06

Learn how to transfer contacts from a MeeToo 4.1

If the contacts on the MeeToo 4.1 are already synchronized. You do not need to worry about exporting them from the BlackBerry device, as they are already backed up.

Synchronize Contacts with MeeToo 4.1

  1. On the MeeToo 4.1 tap Preferences.
  2. Tap Mail, Contacts, Calendars.
  3. Tap Add Account.
  4. Enter the account information.
  5. Tap Confirm.
  6. The Contacts are now synchronized to the MeeToo 4.1.
