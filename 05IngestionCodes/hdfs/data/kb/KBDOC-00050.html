Titanic 1000 - Transfer Contacts

Document Id: KBDOC-00050

Created: 2008-10-21

Updated: 2008-10-31

Learn how to transfer contacts from a Titanic 1000

If the contacts on the Titanic 1000 are already synchronized. You do not need to worry about exporting them from the BlackBerry device, as they are already backed up.

Synchronize Contacts with Titanic 1000

  1. On the Titanic 1000 tap Personalization.
  2. Tap Mail, Contacts, Calendars.
  3. Tap Add Account.
  4. Enter the account information.
  5. Tap Add.
  6. The Contacts are now synchronized to the Titanic 1000.
