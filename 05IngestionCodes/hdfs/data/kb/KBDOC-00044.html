Document Id: KBDOC-00044

Created: 2013-07-02

Updated: 2013-07-10

Learn how to transfer contacts from a iFruit 5

If the contacts on the iFruit 5 are already synchronized. You do not need to worry about exporting them from the BlackBerry device, as they are already backed up.

Synchronize Contacts with iFruit 5

  1. On the iFruit 5 tap Personalization.
  2. Tap Mail, Contacts, Calendars.
  3. Tap Add Account.
  4. Enter the account information.
  5. Tap Add.
  6. The Contacts are now synchronized to the iFruit 5.
