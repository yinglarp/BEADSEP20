MeeToo 2.0 - Change the phone ringtone and notification sound

Document Id: KBDOC-00193

Created: 2010-05-15

Updated: 2010-05-22

Steps to change the phone ringtone for calls on your MeeToo 2.0

  1. From the Preferences screen, tap Preferences.
  2. Tap Sounds.
  3. Tap Ringtone.
  4. Tap your preferred ringtone.
  5. You've now changed the ringtone.

Steps to change the notification ringtone for texts on MeeToo 2.0

  1. From the Preferences screen, tap Preferences.
  2. Tap Sounds.
  3. Tap Text Tone.
  4. Tap your preferred ringtone.
  5. You've now changed the ringtone.

Steps to change the notification ringtone for emails on MeeToo 2.0

  1. From the Preferences screen, tap Preferences.
  2. Tap Sounds.
  3. Scroll to and tap New Mail.
  4. Tap your preferred ringtone.
  5. You've now changed the ringtone.
