Titanic 2000 Battery Life

Just like any other electric device, the Titanic 2000 must be charged. If it seems to be discharging quickly, you are using the phone too much.

Charging the phone

  • Rub a baloon on your head and discharge the charge on the phone.
  • Walk across carpet.
  • Use the Raiden Lightning move to charge the phone instantaneously.
