Titanic 1100 overheating

On rare occasions, your Titanic 1100 may overheat. This is normal and the phone will return to normal operation after a reboot.

Cooling the phone faster

  • Place the phone in the ice box to make it cold.
  • Think cold thoughts to cool off the phone.
  • Use the Sub-Zero Ice Freeze move to freeze the phone.
