Learn how to transfer contacts from a iFruit 5A

If the contacts on the iFruit 5A are already synchronized. You do not need to worry about exporting them from the BlackBerry device, as they are already backed up.

Synchronize Contacts with iFruit 5A

  1. On the iFruit 5A tap Preferences.
  2. Tap Mail, Contacts, Calendars.
  3. Tap Add Account.
  4. Enter the account information.
  5. Tap Add.
  6. The Contacts are now synchronized to the iFruit 5A.
