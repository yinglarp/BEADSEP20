Learn how to transfer contacts from a Sorrento F10L

If the contacts on the Sorrento F10L are already synchronized. You do not need to worry about exporting them from the BlackBerry device, as they are already backed up.

Synchronize Contacts with Sorrento F10L

  1. On the Sorrento F10L tap Settings.
  2. Tap Mail, Contacts, Calendars.
  3. Tap Add Account.
  4. Enter the account information.
  5. Tap Save.
  6. The Contacts are now synchronized to the Sorrento F10L.
