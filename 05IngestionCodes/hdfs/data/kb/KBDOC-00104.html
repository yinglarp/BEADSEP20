Ronin Novelty Note 2 - Transfer Contacts

Document Id: KBDOC-00104

Created: 2011-10-02

Updated: 2011-10-10

Learn how to transfer contacts from a Ronin Novelty Note 2

If the contacts on the Ronin Novelty Note 2 are already synchronized. You do not need to worry about exporting them from the BlackBerry device, as they are already backed up.

Synchronize Contacts with Ronin Novelty Note 2

  1. On the Ronin Novelty Note 2 tap Settings.
  2. Tap Mail, Contacts, Calendars.
  3. Tap Add Account.
  4. Enter the account information.
  5. Tap Add.
  6. The Contacts are now synchronized to the Ronin Novelty Note 2.
