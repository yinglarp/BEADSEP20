iFruit 2 - Change the phone ringtone and notification sound

Document Id: KBDOC-00037

Created: 2010-05-20

Updated: 2010-06-02

Steps to change the phone ringtone for calls on your iFruit 2

  1. From the Personalization screen, tap Settings.
  2. Tap Sounds.
  3. Tap Ringtone.
  4. Tap your preferred ringtone.
  5. You've now changed the ringtone.

Steps to change the notification ringtone for texts on iFruit 2

  1. From the Personalization screen, tap Settings.
  2. Tap Sounds.
  3. Tap Text Tone.
  4. Tap your preferred ringtone.
  5. You've now changed the ringtone.

Steps to change the notification ringtone for emails on iFruit 2

  1. From the Personalization screen, tap Settings.
  2. Tap Sounds.
  3. Scroll to and tap New Mail.
  4. Tap your preferred ringtone.
  5. You've now changed the ringtone.
