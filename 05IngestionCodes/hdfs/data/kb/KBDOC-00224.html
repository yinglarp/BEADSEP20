Updated: 2008-12-05

Learn how to transfer contacts from a Titanic 1100

If the contacts on the Titanic 1100 are already synchronized. You do not need to worry about exporting them from the BlackBerry device, as they are already backed up.

Synchronize Contacts with Titanic 1100

  1. On the Titanic 1100 tap Settings.
  2. Tap Mail, Contacts, Calendars.
  3. Tap Add Account.
  4. Enter the account information.
  5. Tap Save.
  6. The Contacts are now synchronized to the Titanic 1100.
