Learn how to transfer contacts from a Sorrento F01L

If the contacts on the Sorrento F01L are already synchronized. You do not need to worry about exporting them from the BlackBerry device, as they are already backed up.

Synchronize Contacts with Sorrento F01L

  1. On the Sorrento F01L tap Personalization.
  2. Tap Mail, Contacts, Calendars.
  3. Tap Add Account.
  4. Enter the account information.
  5. Tap Confirm.
  6. The Contacts are now synchronized to the Sorrento F01L.
