iFruit 1 - Transfer Contacts

Document Id: KBDOC-00026

Created: 2008-10-21

Updated: 2008-11-02

Learn how to transfer contacts from a iFruit 1

If the contacts on the iFruit 1 are already synchronized. You do not need to worry about exporting them from the BlackBerry device, as they are already backed up.

Synchronize Contacts with iFruit 1

  1. On the iFruit 1 tap Preferences.
  2. Tap Mail, Contacts, Calendars.
  3. Tap Add Account.
  4. Enter the account information.
  5. Tap Add.
  6. The Contacts are now synchronized to the iFruit 1.
