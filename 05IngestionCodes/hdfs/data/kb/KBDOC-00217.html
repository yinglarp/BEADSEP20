Titanic 3000 - Change the phone ringtone and notification sound

Document Id: KBDOC-00217

Created: 2012-08-04

Updated: 2012-08-13

Steps to change the phone ringtone for calls on your Titanic 3000

  1. From the Settings screen, tap Settings.
  2. Tap Sounds.
  3. Tap Ringtone.
  4. Tap your preferred ringtone.
  5. You've now changed the ringtone.

Steps to change the notification ringtone for texts on Titanic 3000

  1. From the Settings screen, tap Settings.
  2. Tap Sounds.
  3. Tap Text Tone.
  4. Tap your preferred ringtone.
  5. You've now changed the ringtone.

Steps to change the notification ringtone for emails on Titanic 3000

  1. From the Settings screen, tap Settings.
  2. Tap Sounds.
  3. Scroll to and tap New Mail.
  4. Tap your preferred ringtone.
  5. You've now changed the ringtone.
