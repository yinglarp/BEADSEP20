Ronin Novelty Note 3 Battery Life

Just like any other electric device, the Ronin Novelty Note 3 must be charged. If it seems to be discharging quickly, you are using the phone too much.

Charging the phone

  • Rub a baloon on your head and discharge the charge on the phone.
  • Find an electric eel.
  • Use the Raiden Lightning move to charge the phone instantaneously.
