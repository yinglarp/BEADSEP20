Sorrento F20L - Change the phone ringtone and notification sound

Document Id: KBDOC-00133

Created: 2010-11-01

Updated: 2010-11-06

Steps to change the phone ringtone for calls on your Sorrento F20L

  1. From the Settings screen, tap Personalization.
  2. Tap Sounds.
  3. Tap Ringtone.
  4. Tap your preferred ringtone.
  5. You've now changed the ringtone.

Steps to change the notification ringtone for texts on Sorrento F20L

  1. From the Settings screen, tap Personalization.
  2. Tap Sounds.
  3. Tap Text Tone.
  4. Tap your preferred ringtone.
  5. You've now changed the ringtone.

Steps to change the notification ringtone for emails on Sorrento F20L

  1. From the Settings screen, tap Personalization.
  2. Tap Sounds.
  3. Scroll to and tap New Mail.
  4. Tap your preferred ringtone.
  5. You've now changed the ringtone.
