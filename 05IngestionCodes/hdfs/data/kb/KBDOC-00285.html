Learn how to back up Ronin S3

Things are backed up to The Cloud! You'll never lose anything!

Some things that are backed up Ronin S3

  • Your call history.
  • Your theme color.
  • The accounts you've set up on your phone.
  • Your bookmark.

To back up Ronin S3

  1. On the Ronin S3 tap Personalization.
  2. Tap Backup.
