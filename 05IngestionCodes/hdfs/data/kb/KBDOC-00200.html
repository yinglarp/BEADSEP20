Learn how to transfer contacts from a Titanic 2000

If the contacts on the Titanic 2000 are already synchronized. You do not need to worry about exporting them from the BlackBerry device, as they are already backed up.

Synchronize Contacts with Titanic 2000

  1. On the Titanic 2000 tap Personalization.
  2. Tap Mail, Contacts, Calendars.
  3. Tap Add Account.
  4. Enter the account information.
  5. Tap Confirm.
  6. The Contacts are now synchronized to the Titanic 2000.
