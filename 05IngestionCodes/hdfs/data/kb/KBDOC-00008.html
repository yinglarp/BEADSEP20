Learn how to transfer contacts from a Titanic 2100

If the contacts on the Titanic 2100 are already synchronized. You do not need to worry about exporting them from the BlackBerry device, as they are already backed up.

Synchronize Contacts with Titanic 2100

  1. On the Titanic 2100 tap Personalization.
  2. Tap Mail, Contacts, Calendars.
  3. Tap Add Account.
  4. Enter the account information.
  5. Tap Add.
  6. The Contacts are now synchronized to the Titanic 2100.
