Titanic 1100 - Change the phone ringtone and notification sound

Document Id: KBDOC-00223

Created: 2008-11-25

Updated: 2008-12-05

Steps to change the phone ringtone for calls on your Titanic 1100

  1. From the Preferences screen, tap Personalization.
  2. Tap Sounds.
  3. Tap Ringtone.
  4. Tap your preferred ringtone.
  5. You've now changed the ringtone.

Steps to change the notification ringtone for texts on Titanic 1100

  1. From the Preferences screen, tap Personalization.
  2. Tap Sounds.
  3. Tap Text Tone.
  4. Tap your preferred ringtone.
  5. You've now changed the ringtone.

Steps to change the notification ringtone for emails on Titanic 1100

  1. From the Preferences screen, tap Personalization.
  2. Tap Sounds.
  3. Scroll to and tap New Mail.
  4. Tap your preferred ringtone.
  5. You've now changed the ringtone.
