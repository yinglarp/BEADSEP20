Learn how to back up Titanic 2000

Things are backed up to The Cloud! You'll never lose anything!

Some things that are backed up Titanic 2000

  • Your call history.
  • Your theme color.
  • The accounts you've set up on your phone.
  • Your bookmark.

To back up Titanic 2000

  1. On the Titanic 2000 tap Preferences.
  2. Tap Backup.
