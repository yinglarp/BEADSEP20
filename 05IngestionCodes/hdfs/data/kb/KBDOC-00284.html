Learn how to transfer contacts from a Ronin S3

If the contacts on the Ronin S3 are already synchronized. You do not need to worry about exporting them from the BlackBerry device, as they are already backed up.

Synchronize Contacts with Ronin S3

  1. On the Ronin S3 tap Personalization.
  2. Tap Mail, Contacts, Calendars.
  3. Tap Add Account.
  4. Enter the account information.
  5. Tap Save.
  6. The Contacts are now synchronized to the Ronin S3.
