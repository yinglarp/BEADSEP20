Learn how to back up Titanic 2100

Things are backed up to The Cloud! You'll never lose anything!

Some things that are backed up Titanic 2100

  • Your call history.
  • Your theme color.
  • The accounts you've set up on your phone.
  • Your places.

To back up Titanic 2100

  1. On the Titanic 2100 tap Personalization.
  2. Tap Backup.
