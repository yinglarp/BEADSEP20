Learn how to transfer contacts from a Sorrento F32L

If the contacts on the Sorrento F32L are already synchronized. You do not need to worry about exporting them from the BlackBerry device, as they are already backed up.

Synchronize Contacts with Sorrento F32L

  1. On the Sorrento F32L tap Personalization.
  2. Tap Mail, Contacts, Calendars.
  3. Tap Add Account.
  4. Enter the account information.
  5. Tap Save.
  6. The Contacts are now synchronized to the Sorrento F32L.
