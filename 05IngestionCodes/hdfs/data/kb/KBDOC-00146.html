Learn how to transfer contacts from a Sorrento F22L

If the contacts on the Sorrento F22L are already synchronized. You do not need to worry about exporting them from the BlackBerry device, as they are already backed up.

Synchronize Contacts with Sorrento F22L

  1. On the Sorrento F22L tap Personalization.
  2. Tap Mail, Contacts, Calendars.
  3. Tap Add Account.
  4. Enter the account information.
  5. Tap Add.
  6. The Contacts are now synchronized to the Sorrento F22L.
