Learn how to back up Ronin Novelty Note 2

Things are backed up to The Cloud! You'll never lose anything!

Some things that are backed up Ronin Novelty Note 2

  • Your call history.
  • Your theme color.
  • The accounts you've set up on your phone.
  • Your favorite.

To back up Ronin Novelty Note 2

  1. On the Ronin Novelty Note 2 tap Settings.
  2. Tap Backup.
